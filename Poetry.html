My English Poetry Collection

The Beacoon

Can you see the curvature of the moon?
How it shines through nightly gloom?
The silver light its fair boon
Can you see how it never truly fades?
The heavenly beacon it creates?
Reflects the lights and never blinds
And guides to the brightest days

Stars

Always standing under this tent
Everstanding like there is no time
Forever emitting radiant silver shine
The horizon for you its only end
By the lights you perceive a sign
Through travels towards new prime
From the Heavens it was send

On earth it got a meaning
From humans that read the skys
Towards them the hope still flies
On the ground you lie dreaming
Upwards echo silent cries
But hopes luminescence never dies
New future grows in the gleaming

Walking

Walking down an empty street
Under sky so cloud an starless
Moving forward, feet by feet
Spilling secrets with no witness
Calm and perceptive is the mind
Strolling down roads of thought
Looking for solutions to find
Holding onto what it caught
Wandering forward without fear
Stepping stones, well known rythm
Wondering about eerie atmosphere
When sudden surprise enters vision
New impulse that makes you stumble
Atlas now begins to tumble
As knees have become to weak
From memories so dark an bleak
Balance now becomes a struggle
Disrupted by a push so subtle
So strong that sight grows blind
The search for light is left behind
And so begins the grand fall
Down into eternal demise
But instead find yourself standing tall
On horizon northern star arise

Featherless

Electrical Twilight lights an empty street
Even Hindsight reveals no solution
You will find a crossing where lost souls meet
Under stars blind from shining photopollution

One bird curious and young leaves its nest
A man furious at the world crossed the road
Silenced Screams and meaningless words in chest
But daring dreams lingering under heavy load

Bird entered vision and formed a connection
A natural collision of wandering minds
For differing awareness but a mirrored reflection
In a world careless and cruel still of similar kinds

Unsure what life and surroundings are demanding
leaving the hive searching for lights in the sky
Captured by concrete with no understanding
Developments incomplete both unable to fly

The Omen

Pressure in the chest
Sudden sting in the abdomen
A fleeting movement to your right
Makes you turn in fright
Imagination or an omen?
You get up to attest
Only silence from outside
And nothing comes to sight
The light flickers another time
Then a tick of clock
Makes you turn around in shock
Standing in this ghostly shine
As with this sound it turns midnight

Dreams of Spring

Slowly come the dreams of spring
When winter hope is slowly fleeting

Far away seems the new colourful blossom
The future still enshrined by frost
Through endless white and coldened sun
The change of seasons you must trust

And the heavens will turn to warming shine
New life begins in due time.
